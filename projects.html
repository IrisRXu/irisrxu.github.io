PillowSense: Smart Fabric for Sleep Monitoring

Research at MobileX Lab, Columbia University

Developed a novel fabric system for non-invasive sleep monitoring with Dr. Xia Zhou, Dr. Qijia Shao, and Lisa Maria DiSalvo. This innovative solution embedds fabric and pressure sensors on a pillowcase to capture biophysiological data during sleep, achieving 92% accuracy in posture classification and clinically acceptable accuracies for heart rate and respiration rate.
